01

The problem

Payment ended the transaction. Nobody thought about what came next, and that's where the value was.

Hospitality staffing shortages directly restricted merchants from capturing critical guest feedback. Dojo's checkout worked, but the experience ended the second a guest paid: reviews, tips and repeat visits were left on the table and we weren't integrating with our restaurant bookings system.

Three squads, one P&L, and a mandate: grow net revenue without breaking a flow people use mid-service.

02

The approach

I

Build the foundation

Built Order & Pay from scratch and rebranded Pay Only, modernising every screen from basket to receipt.

II

Find what works

Ran rapid experiments on the post-payment screens, review prompts, additional tipping, contextual upsell, and scaled what won.

III

Scale to customers

Took the winners live across the customer base, then iterated on their feedback to keep improving.

03

The outcome

Launched QR payments and ran post-payment screen experiments that delivered a 115% uplift in review conversion and a 140% increase in merchant tips within six months.

Across three squads the hospitality vertical delivered 163% YoY net revenue growth on £2.4B of annual card turnover, taking ordering, payments and menu management from research to commercial launch, live across 6+ Epos partners.
