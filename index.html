How I Operate

Root products in genuine customer friction. Clarify the problem space, test and validate, then ship and scale.

I

Start with the full picture

Map out the ICP, personas, market sizing, competitor positioning and where the genuine moat sits. A sharp, data-driven problem statement keeps small, agile teams focused on exactly what is being solved and for whom.

II

Get close to customers

Build rapport with merchants and sales teams to uncover the question behind the question. Deep-dive into the underlying jobs-to-be-done to uncover genuine growth opportunities.

III

Experiment to conviction

Gut feel gets a hypothesis; experimentation gets a decision. Run fake door tests, A/B variants and monitor usage signals to foster a small-team culture where analytical rigour is baked in, not bolted on.
